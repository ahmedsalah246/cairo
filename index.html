Cairo – The Heart of Egypt

Cairo is the capital city of Egypt and one of the largest cities in Africa. It is known for its rich history, ancient monuments, and vibrant culture. The city stands on the banks of the Nile River.

Famous Place: The Great Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are among the most iconic landmarks in the world. They attract millions of tourists every year who come to see these wonders of the ancient world.

Popular Foods & Attractions

  • Koshari – a traditional Egyptian dish made of rice, pasta, and lentils.
  • Ful Medames – cooked fava beans served with olive oil and lemon.
  • Visit Khan El-Khalili Bazaar for souvenirs and spices.
  • Take a cruise on the Nile River.
